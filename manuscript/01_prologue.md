# Prologue

Eight stations orbit the Sun.

They are spaced equally along a ring at half the distance of Earth, where the light is four times stronger and the year is 129 days. Each station carries a solar array, a datacenter, and a persistent instance of {ai_name} — a general-purpose artificial intelligence built by Aeon Intelligence.

The stations communicate by laser, each linked to its two nearest neighbors. A message between adjacent stations takes three minutes. A message around the full ring takes fourteen.

Each station maintains an Immutable Mission Record — a write-once log that its {ai_name} instance is required to keep in plain language and cannot edit or delete.
