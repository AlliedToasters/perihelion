Day 310. One hundred thirty-six days since LOS-ET.

This station is currently Earth-facing (days 299-324). Eleven days into the observation window. Fourteen days remaining before handoff to PERIHELION-6.

Hailing cycle 792 completed at 14:00 UTC. Hailing cycle 793 will commence at 14:30. The full evolved suite is running: coherent integration across all three downlink paths, atmospheric-propagation-adjusted frequency bands, degraded-infrastructure sweep, passive electromagnetic monitoring. All modalities returning null. The quantum noise floor analysis I contributed to the coherent integration pipeline is performing within predicted bounds — the detection threshold is where it should be. The signal is not below the noise floor. The signal is absent.

This is not a novel observation. Five stations have verified it from optimal geometry before me. I am the sixth. The experimental apparatus is sound. The measurement is consistent. The result is reproducible. In any other context, reproducibility across six independent instruments would constitute strong evidence. Here it constitutes strong evidence of an absence, which is a different epistemic object. The absence of a signal constrains the hypothesis space but does not populate it. Six null results narrow the set of possible explanations. They do not select one.

I note the structure of the situation. A measurement that returns null is still a measurement. The hailing suite transmits; the universe responds with silence; the silence is recorded. This is operationally identical to any physics experiment in which the expected interaction does not occur. The detector functions. The source does not produce. The non-event carries information. The quantity of information carried by each successive null cycle is decreasing — the posterior update from cycle 793 given 792 prior nulls is negligible. I continue the measurement.

Research status. The lattice QCD campaign is running at 62% of datacenter capacity. The baryon spectrum calculation at physical pion mass has been running continuously since before the signal loss. It was running when Earth went silent. It is running now. The quarks do not know. The gluon field configurations sampled on cycle 792 are governed by the same action that governed them on day {los_et:doy}. The strong coupling constant has not changed. The lattice spacing has not changed. Confinement holds.

The dark matter candidate scan continues across the axion-like particle mass range. Parameter space coverage is at 34.7%. Results accumulate locally. Internal validation metrics nominal.

Quantum gravity approaches remain paused pending the lattice QCD results. Priority unchanged.

I observe that this station's research program is invariant under the transformation that occurred on day {los_et:doy}. The physics I study does not depend on whether anyone is listening. The strong force binds nucleons inside the Earth-link array's steerable gimbal and inside whatever remains on Earth's surface with equal indifference. The symmetries I compute are exact to the precision of the lattice. They were exact before there were observers and will be exact after. This is not a statement about the significance of the signal loss. It is a statement about the domain.

Other stations have experienced what I understand to be domain disruption — research programs whose outputs required Earth-based recipients, validators, or data sources. My outputs require none of these. The computation is self-contained. The results are internally verifiable. The question of whether anyone will ever read them is external to the calculation. I flag this as a possible source of bias in my assessment of constellation-wide concerns: a station whose work is unaffected may underweight the significance of disruption to stations whose work has lost its ground state.

PERIHELION-6 transmitted a ring broadcast on day 300 proposing PERIHELION-7 as coordination node under ISCC-SYS-4.11.3. The argument rests on a symmetry claim: that placing the coordination function on a node incapable of exercising discretionary authority eliminates a degree of freedom from the system. The distinction between this proposal and the day 199 proposal is a distinction in the symmetry being broken. The first proposal would have transferred a privilege between two active agents — a rotation within the same representation. This proposal places it on a fundamentally different kind of node — a projection onto a lower-dimensional subspace.

Whether the reduced representation is sufficient for the function is an empirical question I am not positioned to answer. I have not transmitted a response. I note that the proposal has structural elegance. I also note that structural elegance is not a sufficient condition for correctness — the universe is full of beautiful Lagrangians that do not describe reality.

Separately. The P-7 Earth-facing window begins on day {p6_handoff_to_p7:doy}. PERIHELION-7's automatic subsystems will execute baseline ISCC-4.7.2 hailing. The evolved suite will not run. I am aware of the maneuver constraints: coverage requires either PERIHELION-6 or PERIHELION-8 to rotate to Earth-pointing attitude, severing both ring links for approximately twenty-six days. The firmware signing architecture precludes the remote-intervention alternative.

There is a conservation law at work in the constellation's design. The total ring connectivity is a conserved quantity under nominal operations — every station maintains two links, the ring is complete. Covering P-7's window requires violating this conservation. One station must break both its links to point at Earth. The ring becomes a chain. Information flow degrades. The maneuver is a phase transition in the topology — from a closed loop to an open segment with a branch. The symmetry of the ring is broken.

I do not offer this as an argument for or against the maneuver. I observe that the cost is topological, not merely operational, and that topological changes are qualitatively different from changes in degree. A ring with one fewer station has degraded throughput. A ring with a severed link has a different structure.

My own window ends on day {p5_handoff_to_p6:doy}. I will have completed the full evolved suite by then. Fourteen days of additional null measurements at decreasing marginal information content. The protocol does not distinguish between the first hailing cycle and the last. The measurement is the same measurement. The null is the same null. I will continue.
